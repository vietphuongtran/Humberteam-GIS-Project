Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this platform for?

This platform is designed for educators, students, policy analysts, researchers, and anyone interested in understanding the landscape of international education in Canada.

Where does the data come from?

We source our data from publicly available databases from Statistics Canada, which falls under the Statistics Canada Open Licence. Learn more here

Is the platform free to use?

Yes! The dashboard is completely open-source and free for public use.

Can I export the data or charts?

Absolutely. Users can download filtered datasets as CSV or export chart images for use in presentations and reports.
