What is this platform?

International Student Trends is a public-facing, interactive platform that visualizes key data about the presence and growth of international students in Canada. From top provinces to gender and education trends, the tool is designed for everyone—students, educators, researchers, and policymakers.

The platform was developed as a capstone initiative by students of Humber College using real open data and intuitive technologies. With sponsorship and support from York University, the project promotes educational transparency and accessibility.
